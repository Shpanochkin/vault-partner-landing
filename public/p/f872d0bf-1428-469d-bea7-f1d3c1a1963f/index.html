Section 3. Volume terms

Commitment, tolerance, and shortfall

Payaq's stated volume commitment defines how the partnership scales. The mechanics below translate the commitment into measurable monthly terms with a tolerance band that accommodates ramp variability.

Monthly volume commitment
€500,000Combined pay-in + pay-out, monthly
The agreed volume floor for the partnership. Calculated across all flows handled under this agreement (USDT pay-in, FX, SEPA / SWIFT pay-out).
Tolerance band
€350K - €500KNo shortfall in this range
Months where actual volume falls between €350,000 and €500,000 incur no shortfall. Covers normal ramp and seasonal variation.
Shortfall fee
2.0% × gapWhen actual < €350,000
If monthly volume drops below €350,000, shortfall fee equals 2.0% of the gap to that threshold. Invoiced at month-end. Example: actual €200K means shortfall = 2.0% × (€350K − €200K) = €3,000.
Platform waiver
€1,000,000Monthly volume threshold
In any month where combined volume exceeds €1,000,000, the monthly platform fee for that month is fully waived.
