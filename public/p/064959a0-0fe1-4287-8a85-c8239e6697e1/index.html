Revenue Model

Where your revenue comes from

As a White Label partner, you capture end-user fees from your branded product. You set end-user prices above the fees Vault charges you. The difference is your margin. You keep all end-user fee revenue and pay Vault the platform fees listed above.

Subscriptions

Plus tier subscriptions at $27.50/mo per user generate predictable recurring revenue that compounds as your user base grows.

Transaction fees

Crypto, FX, SEPA, ATM, and top-up fees accrue with every user action. High-volume users generate revenue continuously.

Your margin

Vault charges a fixed platform fee. All end-user fee revenue above that is yours — no revenue share, no percentage skimmed.

Note on interchange: Interchange revenue from card spending is retained by Vault and is not included in partner revenue. The fee schedule above represents the revenue flows under your control.
