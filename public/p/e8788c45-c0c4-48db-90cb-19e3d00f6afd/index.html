Section 1. How the partnership works

Revenue share, not fees

Vault delivers the full stack: cards, KYC, custody, banking. End-users pay standard rates. That revenue flows into a shared pool, supplier costs come off the top, and what's left splits between Vault and InFlux per the MAU tiers in Section 2.

No fixed costs
No setup fee. No monthly platform fee. Vault's income comes only from its share of net revenue.
Net income, shared
Net income = end-user fee revenue + interchange income − supplier costs. The split between Vault and InFlux follows the MAU tier table below.
Fully aligned
Vault wins when InFlux wins. Active users and transaction volume grow the shared pool. Same incentives for both products.

Section 5. Performance deposit

Mutual commitment on launch

A pure revenue-share model works when both sides commit to ramping the user base. The performance deposit aligns that commitment: refundable on hitting the M3 target, otherwise retained as cost recovery for Vault's setup investment.

Performance deposit
$15,000
Target: 2,000 combined MAU by end of M3
✓ Target achieved
If combined MAU across Zelcore and SSP Wallet reaches 2,000 by end of M3, the $15,000 deposit is refunded in full. Revenue-share continues as the sole compensation structure. No monthly platform fee, ever.
✗ Target missed
If combined MAU is below 2,000 at end of M3, the deposit is retained by Vault and a monthly platform fee of $5,000 activates from M4 onwards. The fee runs until the 2,000 MAU threshold is reached, at which point it turns off and pure revenue-share resumes.
How MAU is counted
MAU (Monthly Active User) = an end-user with an active, paid-for subscription during the calendar month. A user counts toward MAU if their subscription is in good standing at month-end (payment received, not cancelled, not in arrears). Vault publishes the MAU report on the 5th business day of the following month. M3 = third full calendar month after integration go-live.

Deposit paid at SLA signing alongside KYB. Held by Vault, returned via the same method it was received within 14 days of MAU target confirmation.
