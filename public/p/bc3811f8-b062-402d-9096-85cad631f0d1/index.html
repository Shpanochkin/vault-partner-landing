Commercial setup

Tailored to Cheelee's ramp

Custom partnership terms designed around Cheelee's ramp: a refundable security deposit, plus an activation milestone at M3 that determines deposit fate and the billing path going forward.

KYB fee
$500on acceptance
Paid on offer acceptance to start the verification process. Non-refundable — if KYB returns negative, the fee is not returned.
Setup fee
$15,000after KYB + SLA
Paid after KYB approval and SLA signing. Covers contract execution, technical onboarding (sandbox + production keys), and supplier provisioning across the Vault stack.
Security deposit
$10,000refundable on hit
Paid alongside the setup fee, after KYB approval and SLA signing. Held against platform-fee billing. If Cheelee delivers ≥ 1,000 monthly-active users by M3, the deposit is drawn down to cover M3–M6 platform fees. If Cheelee misses, the $10,000 is forfeited and Cheelee pays the full ladder from M3.
Platform fee ladder
$3K → $8Km1–m13+
$3K (M1–M3) → $5K (M4–M6) → $7K (M7–M12) → $8K (M13+). Auto-waived once Vault transactional revenue from Cheelee exceeds $16K/mo (then a 0.2% × TPV floor applies).
M3 activation milestone
1,000monthly active
"Monthly active" = end-user with ≥ 1 transactional operation in the calendar month. M1–M2 are unconditional grace months. M3 is the first evaluation point — the outcome determines the entire Y1 economics.
